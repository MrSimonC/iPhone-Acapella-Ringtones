Free iPhone Ringtones

How to install on your iPhone:
  1. Download the .m4r file to your computer.
  2. Open iTunes (Windows) or Finder (Mac).
  3. Connect your iPhone with a cable.
  4. Drag the .m4r file into the Tones section.
  5. Sync your iPhone.
Note: Direct installation from Safari or your iPhone browser is not supported by Apple.
